Blood Sugar Logbook

Another android app! This one helps diabetics keep track of their blood sugars and see the ups and downs over time. It uses a SQLite database to store the data. The glucose numbers are color coded based on certain blood sugar ranges to visually show highs and lows.

I have had type 1 diabetes since I was 5 years old. I was inspsired to create this app as a tool myself and others to gain better control of our blood sugars.
